AMPHITHEATRE

The amphitheatre is a place where you can come and enjoy an educational and entertaining event. It is a great place to learn about different animals, their habitats, and their behaviors. The amphitheatre hosts various shows and presentations throughout the day, making it a perfect spot for families and animal lovers alike.

The amphitheatre is located in the heart of the ZOO, surrounded by lush greenery and beautiful gardens. It provides a comfortable seating area for visitors to relax and enjoy the shows. The amphitheatre is also equipped with state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems to enhance the overall experience.

Whether you are interested in learning about the majestic elephants, playful monkeys, or colorful birds, the amphitheatre has something for everyone. The knowledgeable staff and trainers provide insightful commentary during the shows, making it an educational experience as well.

So, if you are looking for a fun and informative way to spend your day at the zoo, be sure to check out the amphitheatre. It is a must-visit attraction that will leave you with lasting memories and a deeper appreciation for the animal kingdom.

OPERATING HOURS: 10:00-14:00
