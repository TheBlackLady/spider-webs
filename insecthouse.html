INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is a fascinating place to explore the world of insects. It features a variety of exhibits showcasing different species, their habitats, and their roles in the ecosystem. Visitors can learn about the importance of insects in pollination, decomposition, and as a food source for other animals.

The Insect House also includes interactive displays, educational programs, and guided tours to enhance the learning experience. It's a great place for families and school groups to discover the incredible diversity of insects and their contributions to our environment.

OPERATING HOURS: 09:00-17:00
