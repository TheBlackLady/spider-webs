MONKEYS

The dusky leaf monkey, spectacled langur, or spectacled leaf monkey (Trachypithecus obscurus) is a species of primate in the family Cercopithecidae. It is found in Malaysia, Burma, and Thailand.

They are known for their distinctive facial markings and playful behavior. They are primarily arboreal, spending most of their time in trees, and are known for their acrobatic skills.Dusky leaf monkeys are herbivores, primarily feeding on leaves, fruits, and flowers.

They are social animals, living in small groups typically consisting of a few adults and their young.Dusky leaf monkeys are known for their loud vocalizations, which they use to communicate with each other.They are listed as a vulnerable species due to habitat loss and hunting.

Dusky leaf monkeys are also known for their unique grooming behavior, where they use their long tails to help clean each other's fur.

Dusky leaf monkeys are diurnal, meaning they are active during the day and rest at night. Dusky leaf monkeys have a gestation period of about 5-6 months, after which a single infant is born.
