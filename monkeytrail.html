MONKEY-TRAIL

Welcome to the Monkey Trail, a delightful path where you can observe playful monkeys in their natural habitat. This trail offers a unique opportunity to see these fascinating creatures up close as they swing from tree to tree and interact with each other. It's a perfect spot for nature enthusiasts and families looking for an adventurous outing.

As you walk along the trail, you'll be surrounded by lush greenery and the sounds of the forest. Keep your eyes peeled for various species of monkeys, each with its own unique behaviors and characteristics. The Monkey Trail is not just a walk; it's an experience that brings you closer to wildlife and nature.

Whether you're a photographer looking to capture the perfect shot or simply want to enjoy a peaceful stroll, the Monkey Trail has something for everyone. Don't forget to bring your camera and binoculars for an unforgettable experience!
